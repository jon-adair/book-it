Neil Armstrong

Neil Armstong also known as the first man on the moon. On August 5, 1930 Neil was born in Wapakoneta, Ohio. At age 14 he started taking flying lessons, and at age 16 on his birthday he was issued his pilots licence. He had built a small air tunnel in his basement at home (a tunnel used to study the effect of air flow) he also studied his model planes. At a young age he wanted to work in aeronautics, or construction, design, and aircraft control. He also became interested in outer space, then in July 1969 he stated the famous words "one small step for man, one giant leap for mankind".
