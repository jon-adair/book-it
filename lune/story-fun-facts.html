Fun Facts About the Moon

  • The dark side of the moon is a myth (not true) all of the moon receives the same amount of sunlight just that the earth doesn't see all of the moon
  • Waves in the ocean are caused by the moon because of its gravitational pull (think of it like a magnet and the water is the thing getting pulled by the magnet)
  • The moon is getting further from the earth it is drifting 3.8 cm every year in 50 billion years the moon will take 47 years to orbit the earth instead of the current 27.3 days now
  • The moon has been walked by 12 people all american males last time in 1972 since then all unmanned vehicles have visited the moon
  • The first craft to land on the moon was the Luna 1 in 1959
  • If all goes to plan the next person to walk on the moon will be in 2019
  • During the 1950s the USA considered detonating a nuclear bomb on the moon to show power
